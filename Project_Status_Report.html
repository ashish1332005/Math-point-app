2. Completed Features Detail

👨‍💼 Admin Panel

  • Dashboard: High-level overview and analytics of the platform.
  • Course Management: Unified premium course cards, centralized course details.
  • Content Management: Publishing videos and study materials restricted to specific courses.
  • Student Management: Managing student enrollments and data.
  • Attendance Management: Tracking and updating student attendance records.
  • Financials: Tracking fee payments and orders.

🎓 Student Portal

  • Dashboard: Personalized learning overview, "My Learning" and "Quick Access" cards.
  • Course & Lesson Viewing: Immersive, categorized learning experience. Secure video delivery with anti-piracy features (dynamic watermarks, disabled right-click).
  • Academics: Tracking personal attendance, viewing exam results, and accessing test series.
  • Financials: Viewing fee status and past purchases.

👪 Parent Portal

  • Dashboard: Overview of connected children.
  • Children Management: Viewing profiles of linked student accounts.
  • Attendance Tracking: Monitoring children's class attendance.

👨‍🏫 Teacher Portal

  • Dashboard & Courses: Quick overview of assigned duties and courses.
  • Student Access: Viewing students enrolled in their courses.

🌐 Public Website (Guest)

  • Pages: Home, About, Contact, Faculties.
  • Course Discovery: Public-facing course cards that sync with admin data.
  • Authentication Flow: Role-based portal selection landing page.

3. Pending Work (What's Next)

  • Live Classes Module: Integration with Zoom API, Agora, or Google Meet.
  • Payment Gateway Integration: Integration with Razorpay/Stripe for automated fee collection.
  • Assignment & Homework Submission: Portal for students to upload assignments.
  • Automated Communication: SMS/Email gateways to send automated OTPs and alerts.
